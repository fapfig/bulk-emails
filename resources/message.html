ANNOUNCEMENT OF RESEARCH GRANT FOR POSTDOC


The Structural Composites Research group of the Institute for Sustainability and Innovation in Structural Engineering (ISISE), University of Minho, Portugal, is opening a call to award three (3) research grants within the R&D Project “RoboShot@FRC - Robotized system for the shotcrete of optimized fibre reinforced concrete in railway tunnels”, with reference POCI-01-0247-FEDER-047075, financed by FEDER through Operational Competitiveness and Internationalization Program (COMPETE 2020), in Civil Engineering or related fields. A candidate can apply for more than one grant, but if selected, only one grant will be attributed to the candidate. The application deadline is 19/01/2022 (23:00 – Europe/London) and must be submitted by email for the addresses indicated in the full announcement. 

The full announcement can be downloaded at https://euraxess.ec.europa.eu/jobs/725474
