ANNOUNCEMENT TO AWARD A RESEARCH GRANT TO A PHD STUDENT

The Structural Composites Research group of the Institute for Sustainability and Innovation in Structural Engineering (ISISE), University of Minho, Portugal, is opening a research grant for PhD Students to develop research activities within the I&D Project SLABIMP, co-financed by FEDER through Operational Competitiveness and Internationalization Program (POCI).

The research activities: development of fibre reinforced concrete (FRC) for prefabricated prestressed beams and for lightweight blocks for prefabricated slabs, as well as on the experimental programs to be carried out for the assessment of the mechanical and durability properties of these materials and construction systems.

Necessary qualifications: MSc degree in Civil Engineering or related field (mandatory).

Recipients: Students of a PhD Programme in Civil Engineering at the University of Minho or candidate with master degree enrolled in a non-degree courses within the educational offer of the University of Minho (proof of registration for any of the situations should be provided at the time of the contract processing)

Application deadline and form of submission of applications: The call is open from 27/05/2021 to 11/06/2021.

Applications must be made through an application letter together with the following documents: Curriculum Vitae, certificates of graduation degrees with grades (or additional transcript of grades) or a Declaration of Honour; document proving enrollment in PhD (if applicable at the date of application); letter of interest.

All applications must be submitted by email to the following address: barros@civil.uminho.pt, with the following subject: “SLABIMP - POCI-01-0247-FEDER-033883-UM.1.21”.
